My Background

Accounting -- Financial Services

My love for programming started back in my accounting days when I was 21 (2008). A large part of my job was building and maintaing sophistated Excel spreadsheets that needed to perform a variety of functions. I thoroughly enjoyed taking an idea from concept to reality within this program for both accounting and operational needs. People advised along the way that I should seriously consider getting into programming, but unfortunately I didn't listen at the time. In 2016, I decided to move on and get a start in the insurance business as an agent/financial representative. There were some things I enjoyed about this career, but I couldn't get away from my base passion which was programming. I really missed the Excel stuff, but didn't want to go back into accounting. I decided it was take to jump into the world of software engineering and I definitely see this is where I belong.

Here is a link to learn more if you're interested: wakingup.com. I also plan on taking a meditation retreat in the near future.
